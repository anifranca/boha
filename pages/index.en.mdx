# BOHA: Environmental History Bibliography

**BOHA** is a collaborative bibliography curated by the
Latin American and Caribbean Society for Environmental History.
We focus on academic works available in Portuguese, English and Spanish,
covering major research topics the field. Our data is organized in Zotero
and our contributors assure we have reliable citations for all works.

If you would like to join us, please read our [contributing guidelines](/contribute).
